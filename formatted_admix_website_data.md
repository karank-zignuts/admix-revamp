## Company Overview

Admix Metacraft LLP began as a small engineering workshop started by two young entrepreneurs to provide machining solutions. Over time, the company developed its own products and started offering customized engineering solutions based on market needs.

The company started manufacturing FRP manhole covers under the WAVE brand in 2018. In 2021, it introduced additional products including main door handles and glass door handles. The company focuses on customer satisfaction, competitive pricing, service quality, and product quality.

## Product: FRP Manhole Covers

### Description

FRP manhole covers are presented as an alternative to Cast Iron, Ductile Iron, and RCC manhole covers. The product is described as an industrial improvement and is stated to be tested as per BS EN 124-1994 standards on the product page.

The About page also mentions testing as per BS EN 124-1-2015 standards for drainage and utility services, and states that the manhole covers are tested and approved by MSME as per standards.

### Load Capacity

Available with load bearing capacity ranging from 2.5 tons to 5 tons.

### Features

- Theft proof because covers do not contain metallic or plastic material.
- Specially fabricated top layer to resist high abrasion.
- UV stabilized pigments for sunlight resistance.
- Zero permanent set in all sizes and load bearings.
- SS 304 rod and ring combination provided for lifting.
- Non-brittle construction.
- Locking arrangement available on request.
